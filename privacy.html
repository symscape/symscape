Privacy Policy

Symscape has created this policy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following sections disclose the information gathering and dissemination practices for Symscape’s website and products.

Website

IP addresses of visitors to our website may be used to help diagnose problems with our server and to administer our website. IP addresses may also be used to compile general demographic information. We will not attempt to identify individual users through their IP addresses.

Cookies

Cookies are used by nearly all major websites to store session information that enables easy access to shopping carts, recent searches, etc.

A cookie is a small amount of data that is sent to your browser from a website and stored on your computer in a file. Each website can send its own cookie to your browser, but only the site that sent a particular cookie can read it.

You can set your browser to accept all cookies, to not store any cookies, or to notify you when a cookie is set. (See your browser's Help menu for information about setting your cookie preferences.) If your browser is set to reject all cookies, you will be able to browse symscape.com anonymously, but you will not be able to take full advantage of all the symscape.com features.

To become a registered Symscape member, you will need to set your browser to accept our cookie. Symscape uses cookies to

  • Access your information when you log into symscape.com so we can display your account details
  • Save any preferences you specify when using symscape.com
  • Require you to periodically log back into symscape.com to prevent access to your symscape.com account by others

Order/Registration Forms

Our site and our programs use order forms for customers to register or request information, products, and services. We collect contact information (such as your email address), but NOT financial information (such as account or credit card numbers). Any credit card processing is managed by certified third parties.

Symscape does not sell or use your private information for any purpose other than to:

  • Email your order or free trial license keys
  • Contact you if there is a problem with your order or free trial
  • Keep you informed of Symscape products
