What is product activation?

Submitted by Alison Findon on September 7, 2006 - 09:06

Product activation aims to ensure legitimate software use i.e., to reduce software piracy, by ensuring that a single subscription is tied to a single computer. Reduced piracy means that we can invest more in product development, quality and support, ultimately to the benefit of you our customers.

The activation process is as follows:

  1. Download and install Caedium for your platform.
  2. Purchase a subscription(s) or request a free trial – an activation license(s) will be sent to your registered email address
  3. Run Caedium and in the Help Toolbar click Activate to launch the Product Activation wizard
  4. Copy and paste (Windows & Linux: Ctrl+C then Ctrl+V, Mac: Cmd+C then Cmd+V) your license key from your activation email into the wizard (shown below)
  5. Restart Caedium to use the newly activated add-on(s)

Product Activation using a License Key

During the final step in the activation process, the activation license key and a unique one-way computer hash, or checksum, are sent to our activation server, which validates the information and activates the product on your computer. No personally-identifying data is included or required.

Note that you may purchase the same subscription for multiple computers.

Email Activation Process

Submitted by symscape on April 2, 2012 - 15:14.

Yes, the process goes as follows:

  1. Copy and paste your activation license email into a file
  2. Transfer the file to your Caedium computer
  3. On your Caedium computer, in the Help Toolbar click Activate
  4. Select Activation Method = Email (slow), click Next
  5. Select Activation and copy and paste the activation license from the email file, click Next
  6. Copy and paste the email fields from the Caedium activation wizard into a new file
  7. Transfer the new file to your computer with email access
  8. Copy and paste the email fields from the new file in to an email and send the email
  9. In return we will email you the runtime activation license
  10. Copy the runtime activation email to a file
  11. Transfer the file to your Caedium computer
  12. On your Caedium computer click Help->Activate Product
  13. Select Activation Method = Email (slow), click Next
  14. Select Runtime and copy and paste the runtime license from the email file, click Next
  15. Should be all set to run Caedium
