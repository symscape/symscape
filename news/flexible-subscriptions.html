Introducing Flexible Caedium Subscriptions

Submitted by symscape on April 23, 2010 - 12:22
  • News

Caedium subscriptions are now available per month, per quarter, and per year for small businesses and hobbyists to perform Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) simulations as needed. The new subscriptions bring CFD within the budget of anyone who wants to join the virtual simulation revolution.

The flexible Caedium subscriptions range from per month to per year with no minimum duration and no startup fees. Whether you are an intermittent casual user or an all-year-round power user, it's easy to find a Caedium subscription to match your needs.

Affordable subscriptions to Caedium add-ons expand the free Caedium viewer into a complete Computer-Aided Engineering (CAE) analysis tool for performing state-of-the-art CFD simulations. All the Caedium add-ons seamlessly integrate within the same easy-to-use unified simulation environment.

And don't forget, whether your favorite operating system is Windows, Mac OS X, or Linux, Caedium has you covered with its unique native cross-platform architecture.

See Caedium Product Comparisons for the new prices and a feature list of each add-on.
